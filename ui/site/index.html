How Does CareTraq Work?

  • You purchase the CareTraq Package that works best for your company. We provide the CareTraq Software and a tracking decal.
  • Each time a patient is vistied the health care worker clicks the decal, inputs the necessary information from the visit, and uploads to CareTraq's Servers.
  • Real-Time data is shown on your CliqCare dashboard. This information can be shared with anyone you chose, including the patient's family.

Frequently Asked Questions

If I purchase the SilverCare Package can I update to Gold, or Platinum as my company grows?
Of course you can. We are able to upgrade your company any time. You would pay an upgrade fee, the new per patient cost and the upgrade is complete.
Who gets access to the information that is collected daily?
A custom dashboard is created for each company. With the platinum package you are given unlimited access to this page. You are able to give access to anyone from employees, to patient families. CareTraq gives you, The Admin, the ability to limit what each user sees. This gives you the ability to control what information is accessed, while giving the Patient's family the peace of mind that has been unseen in the industry to date.
What gives you the ability to track in real-time?
For years families have wanted a way to check on their loved ones. We were able to leverage the use of NFC (Near Feild Communication), the cloud, and geo-locations in a way that provides real-time data giving families the peace of mind they have been begging for.
What is your cancellation policy?
CliqCare requires a 30 day notice prior to cancellation.
What happens if the number of patients I am tracking grows, or decreases month to month?
We bill on a month to month basis which means there is no need to worry about this. At the end of the month we asses how many patients were tracked and you are billed accordingly.
